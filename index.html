Carlos Alcaraz achieves Career Grand Slam at the Australian Open

Bernarda Malevska

Carlos Alcaraz defeated Novak Djokovic in four sets on Sunday in Melbourne, becoming the youngest man in tennis history to achieve a Career Grand Slam by winning titles at all four major tournaments. The world No. 1 player joins a group of just eight others who have accomplished the feat.

The 22-year-old captured his seventh Grand Slam title, as he holds two titles at each of the three other Grand Slams.
